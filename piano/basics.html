Now it's time for some of my worded tutoring.


  • The first key on the piano all the way to your left starts at the letter A.
  • The Piano scale only goes up to G so start couting the letters from A-G from the first key to the key that G ends on.
  • After you reach the G then repeat starting with the same key that ended with G that you left off on.
  • Now that you got the lettering right!
  • Here, I think the chromatic scale on the Piano would really help you in mastering this technique and remembering the keys.


The "Chromatic Scale technique is clicking on A,B,C,D,E,F,G again and again till you reach the last key of the piano which is all the way to the right.
